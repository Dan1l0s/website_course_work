Modern OS
Operating system, known as OS, is a complex of control and processing programs that, on the one hand,, they act as an interface between computing system devices and application programs, and on the other hand — designed for device management, computing process management, efficient distribution computing resources between computing processes and the organization of reliable computing.

This site is an archive of modern operating systems used by ordinary users, in which everything is described in extreme detail currently existing popular operating systems among users.
